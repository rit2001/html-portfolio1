The Best Movies According to Ritwik

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Eternal Sun-Shine of the Spotless Mind

This is my favourite pshychological,romantic love story and science fiction movie. I love the wonderful story line of the this movie.

Water: The Great Mistry

Scientists, philosophers and theologians try to understand water's explicit and implicit properties beyond the common physical laws of nature.

Life Is Beautiful

Life is Beautiful is introduced as a fable. From a Dramatica perspective, it contains two grand argument stories; a boy meets girl romantic comedy, followed by a father and son tragicomedy.
